The intersection between technology and human cognition has always fascinated me. What can we learn from the brain that we can apply to technology? How does technology impact human behavior? How can we harness the power of technology to help people make positive changes in their lives? These are the questions that inspire me.

I was drawn to the Internet as a medium of communication before I went away to academy. Just the idea that I could create something online that other people could see, my own website, was strangely compelling to me. I built my first website using Yahoo! GeoCities when I was fourteen years old. Since that day, I've continued to learn new skills related to web development and design.
